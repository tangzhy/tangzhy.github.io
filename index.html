About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Benyou Wang and co-supervised by Ming Yan. My research focuses on agentic systems built with large language models, especially systems that can perceive, reason, and act in real environments.

I am especially interested in coding agents, computer-use agents, and phone-use agents, with a focus on making them more capable, reliable, and privacy-aware in practical settings.

I have had the opportunity to work with teams including Tencent Hunyuan, Moonshot AI (Kimi), Qwen, and Microsoft Research Asia.
